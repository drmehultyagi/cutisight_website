Transforming Dermatology with AI

A vision by Dr. Mehul Tyagi, MD (AIIMS Delhi)

Our Vision for India

Bridging the Gap

Enable peripheral healthcare workers to deliver dermatological care with AI assistance, reducing dependency on specialist availability in rural areas.

Workflow Efficiency

Reduce consultation times by 60% through AI-powered preliminary assessments, allowing dermatologists to focus on complex cases.

Accessible Technology

Offline-capable, low-resource AI models designed specifically for Indian skin types and tropical dermatological conditions.

Interdepartmental Support

Assist non-dermatology departments in identifying skin manifestations of systemic diseases during inpatient consults.
